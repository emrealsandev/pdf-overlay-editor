Privacy Policy for PDF Overlay Editor

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

We respect your privacy. The PDF Overlay Editor extension is designed to work entirely within your browser.

Data Collection and Usage
- We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data.
- All PDF files and annotations you create are processed and stored locally on your computer.
- The extension does not send any information about your browsing activity or the files you work with to our servers or any third party.

Permissions
The permissions requested by the extension are solely for its core functionality (such as opening PDF files in the editor and saving your settings locally) and are not used to track you or collect your data.
